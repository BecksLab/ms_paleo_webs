Model choice influences inferred extinction dynamics

Figure S3. Temporal trajectories of network-level metrics across reconstruction models.

Macro (connectance, trophic level), Meso (motif counts: linear chains, omnivory, direct and apparent competition), and Micro (generality, vulnerability). Lines represent mean values across replicates. Deterministic, size-based models (ADBM, L-matrix) show highly similar trajectories, whereas stochastic or trait-driven models (log ratio, niche, random) exhibit greater divergence in both mean values and temporal patterns, consistent with GAM analyses (see main text).
